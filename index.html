Movement Controls

Use Keyboard W-A-S-D, ARROW KEYS, or the interactive virtual d-pad on touch screens.

Audio

Toggle the speaker icon for sound effects and ambient background music during gameplay. Music pauses when the game is paused.

Dynamic Game Modes

  • Classic: Keep strictly inside bounds. Any border collision is instant failure.
  • Portal: Borders seamlessly loop the snake around the screen.
  • Labyrinth: Procedurally generated walls each run — route carefully around shifting obstacles.

VS Rival CPU Mode

Compete against an intelligent opponent on the same board! Race to collect coins first, avoid crashing into the CPU's tail, and secure points to win the match. You can set the CPU intensity to Easy, Medium, Hard, or Ultra!

Solo Pace

Relaxed, Standard, or Aggressive sets base snake speed and how fast difficulty ramps as your score climbs.

Combo Streak

Eat coins within 2.5 seconds of the last bite to stack combo multipliers (up to 2x bonus points).

Buffered Nanites (Powerups)

  • 🛡️ GHOST (Blue): Phase harmlessly through your own snake body segments.
  • 🐢 FREEZE (Yellow): Dilates time, slowing your progression down for better execution.
  • ✨ MULTIPLIER (Pink): Activates Double points matrix multiplier for limited durations.
  • 📉 SHRINK (Violet): Instantly trims up to 3 tail segments (minimum length 3).
